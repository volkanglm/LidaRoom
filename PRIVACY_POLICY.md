# Privacy Policy — LidaRoom

**Last updated: March 15, 2026**

---

## English

### 1. Overview

LidaRoom ("the App") is developed and maintained by Volkan Gülem ("Developer," "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

**LidaRoom does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the Developer or any third party.**

### 2. Data We Collect and How It Is Stored

| Data | Where It Is Stored | Shared? |
|---|---|---|
| Room scan data (walls, doors, windows, furniture positions) | Locally on your device (SwiftData) | Never |
| App preferences (measurement unit, language, color scheme) | Locally on your device (UserDefaults) | Never |
| Purchase status (lifetime unlock) | Locally on your device (Keychain) | Never |
| Scan and export counters | Locally on your device (Keychain) | Never |

All data created by LidaRoom stays on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers (we have no servers), third-party analytics services, or advertising networks.

### 3. Camera and LiDAR Sensor

LidaRoom requires access to your device's camera and LiDAR sensor exclusively to perform room scanning using Apple's RoomPlan framework. No images, videos, or sensor data are stored or transmitted beyond the structured scan result (wall positions, dimensions, and object categories) which is saved only on your device.

### 4. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for information about how Apple handles payment data.

### 5. Third-Party Services

LidaRoom uses the following Apple frameworks. No third-party SDKs or analytics services are integrated:

- **Apple RoomPlan** – LiDAR-based room scanning
- **Apple StoreKit 2** – In-app purchases
- **Apple SwiftData** – Local data persistence
- **Apple SceneKit** – 3D model rendering

### 6. Children's Privacy

LidaRoom does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect personal information from any user.

### 7. Data Retention and Deletion

All scan data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual scans within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored App data.

### 8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. For any concerns, contact us at the address below.

### 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### 10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
